Tell a story without words


Group work


[Communication and presentation] [Design and visualisation] [Interaction development]


The task was to tell a story in images alone. No words could be spoken in the video.


The story must be telling a story from one of the following general themes:

  • Urban living
  • People who are too busy
  • Difficult liaisons

We chose people who are too busy and I worked on the story line and the filming of certain scenes.
